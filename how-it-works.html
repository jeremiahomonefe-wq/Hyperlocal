Common questions

Got questions?
We've got answers.

When is Hyperlocal launching? +
We're in the final stages of development and pilot preparation. Join the waitlist and you'll be among the very first to know — and to order — when we go live in Abraka and Warri.
Do I need to download an app? +
At launch, you'll be able to order via our mobile app (iOS and Android) or through our website. During the early pilot phase, some orders may be placed via WhatsApp while the full app is being finalised.
How much does delivery cost? +
A flat ₦500 delivery fee on every order. No surge pricing. No hidden charges. What you see in your cart is exactly what you pay.
What if something is wrong with my order? +
Contact us via WhatsApp within 30 minutes of delivery and we'll sort it. Wrong item, missing item, or quality issue — we'll make it right, either with a replacement or a credit to your account.
Can I schedule a delivery for later? +
Scheduled deliveries are on our roadmap and will be available in Phase 2 of the platform. At launch, all deliveries are on-demand — placed now, delivered as fast as possible.
